Tech Turtles - Outreach/Fundraisers

Each year, after the competition season ends, the Tech Turtles contribute to the preservation and growth of FIRST and their own team through community outreach.

FTC Open House - Outreach


At our FTC Open House, we schedule a date to present to those interested in joining the major aspects of our team at Okaloosa S.T.E.M.M. Academy. For example, we tell them about functions such as E-book, programming, and building of our team.

Thrift Sale - Fundraising

June 7 - 8, 2019, we held a thrift sale at O.S.A. to raise funds for parts for our robot. We sold off donations from parents and coaches, unneeded items such as gently used lunchboxes and blankets at a reduced price.
